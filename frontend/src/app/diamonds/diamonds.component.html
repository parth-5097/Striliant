Specifications

Kukadia Specifications classify diamonds into different quality groups for easy classification. They are for Round shape laboratory graded diamonds and are based on a combination of measurements, finish and other physical diamond characteristics. The Rapaport Price List is based on RapSpec A3 and better diamonds.

Eye Clean

”Eye Clean” means that:

No inclusions (black, white, red or any other color) can be seen by a non-expert grader when viewing the diamond from a face-up position (looking at the table) with the naked eye.

Eye clean does not include looking at the diamond under any magnification (loupe, microscope etc…) or instrumentation.
