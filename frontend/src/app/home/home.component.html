Infrastructure

Kukadia Group is a professionally run company that pays key attention to the infrastructure and amenities to outgrow sustainably. We have a Solid Manufacturing Facility with a confluence of sophisticated tools and machinery to craft conflict-free diamonds with the best polishing work. Our team of 950+ craftsmen is extensively involved in turning every stone into a gem of its kind in compliance with international standards and rules.

With state-of-the-art software and technologies, we smoothen the rough edges and take every piece of diamond to a whole other level by employing cutting, bruting and automated polishing machines to the production process. Thus, progress in infrastructure means progress in the diamond industry where you can bring phenomenal products on the market and help miners, manufacturers, traders, retailers, and consumers to obtain the best products, with the most efficiency.

At Kukadia Group, we endeavor to be the best by providing crystal clear quality that ensures customer satisfaction in all aspects.
