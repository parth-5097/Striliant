Diamond Clarity refers to the absence of hues, inclusions, and blemishes within or on a diamond when viewed under 10x magnification. At Kukadia Group, we follow the GIA Clarity Scale ranging from flawless (FL) to (I3).

  • Flawless (FL)

    Neither inclusions nor blemishes are visible to the professional grader using 10× magnification

  • Internally Flawless (IF)

    Only blemishes are visible and not inclusions to a skilled grader using 10× magnification

  • Very, Very Slightly Included (VVS1 and VVS2)

    It is quite challenging for a skilled grader to view the Inclusions under 10× magnification.

  • Very Slightly Included (VS1 and VS2)

    Inclusions are minor and range from difficult to somewhat easy for a skilled grader to see under 10x magnification.

  • Slightly Included (SI1 and SI2)

    Inclusions are noticeable to a skilled grader under 10x magnification.

  • Included (I1, I2, and I3)

    Inclusions are obvious under 10× magnification and may affect transparency and brilliance.
