Round Cut Diamond

Round Cut Diamonds are one of the most ideal choices for engagement rings that spark up the dazzling glow with their unique mechanism of shape. This round cut has been specially engineered to reflect the brilliance and dispersion with intense brightness. Approximately, 75% of all the round cut diamonds are sold showing its popularity widely.

Emerald Cut Diamond

Using the original technique for the emerald cuts, these are usually rectangular in shape, often distinguished by beveled corners and step facets. With long and straight lines, it offers abundant reflections making Emerald Cut Diamond a premium choice with lesser spark and more quality and clarity.

This diamond shape produces the hall of the mirror effect that interacts with the dark and light planes. It gives you a vintage thought and elevates the look and feel of your slender finger. Accounting for only 3% of the diamond market, it is considered as one of the least common diamond cuts compared to round cut diamonds.

Cushion Cut Diamond

Introduced back in the early 1800s, it has a luxurious history reflecting a more traditional outlook with the less fiery and more brilliant for any special occasion. Cushion Cut Diamonds have rounded corners, cut into a square and rectangular shape, having a precise binding and dispersion of light. These are available in several variations like- Modern, Standard and Modified differ with the uniqueness in designs.

Cushion Cut Diamonds always have the luxurious appeal due to their larger facets and intense flashes of fire, giving a slimming effect on the fingers. Also, if you want to display the color diamonds, Cushion Cut Diamonds are the best options for your requirement.
